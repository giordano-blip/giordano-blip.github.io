About

I'm a Brazilian-Polish student from São Paulo, currently completing the IB Diploma at St. Paul's School with Higher Level subjects in Physics, Computer Science, and Mathematics. I will be joining Lafayette College in the United States, having been accepted through Early Decision.

My intellectual interests sit at the junction of physics, computing, and education. I've explored everything from the luminous efficacy of LEDs for my Extended Essay, to building mathematical models of rowing boat motion using higher derivatives and real Championship data.

Beyond the classroom, I believe technology is most powerful when it's shared. I've led Code Kids — a community program teaching Python to students in a state school in Vila Madalena — since 2022, and have taught programming across multiple initiatives including JuntosNaTech and an AI & Machine Learning enrichment course.

Outside of tech and science, I'm a competitive rower, a choir singer, and a Kung Fu practitioner — disciplines that have each taught me something about precision, patience, and the pursuit of mastery.
